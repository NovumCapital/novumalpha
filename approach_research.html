Research

Better Data Delivers Better Performance

Much of the data that exist in cryptocurrency markets is noise and has limited impact on trading performance. By leveraging our proprietary data science tools, we are able to synthesize uncorrelated data points to develop accurate and timely trading overlays through our constant research and development of quantitative trading models.

Given the poor quality of data available in cryptocurrency markets, our proprietary data analytics tools provide us with an unparalleled advantage in sifting out outcome-oriented data and our data interpretation tools ensure this data can then be transposed into trading ideas.

The core objective of the research team is to identify data points and to study their relationships with cryptocurrency price movements, to develop cohesive allocation models that are resilient in all market conditions and against the backdrop of constantly varying data points and datasets.

Research Methodology

Researchers are responsible for using statistical analytical tools to identify market inefficiencies and trading opportunities and to use the results of their research to assess trading strategies for efficacy and consistency.

We take an inductive approach towards our trading research and delve deep into the underlying blockchain technology of the cryptocurrencies we actively trade.
