Allocation Objective

Novum Global Technologies specializes in growing your digital asset portfolio by trading in cryptocurrencies using our systematic absolute return strategies to generate consistent, uncorrelated returns on digital assets.

We strongly believe in diversifying risks inherent in cryptocurrency trading to preserve the value of your cryptocurrency assets as well as to generate returns on those digital assets. Portfolio risk is minimized through extensive diversification across a range of liquid and well-traded cryptocurrencies as well as by making a large number of small trades.

Risk is also managed at the portfolio level using a suite of both automated portfolio management and monitoring tools, as well as regular managerial oversight.

Automated risk management algorithms are designed to stop out trades when preset risk parameters are exceeded and portfolio performance is managed 24/7 by risk management software.
